Royal Challengers Bangalore

About Us

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a professional cricket franchise based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. The team competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and is known for its strong fan base and exciting style of play.

Legacy

RCB has been part of the IPL since 2008 and is famous for its loyal supporters and memorable matches.

Players

The team has featured legendary players like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle.

Vision

The mission of RCB is to play bold cricket, entertain fans, and bring the IPL trophy to Bengaluru.
